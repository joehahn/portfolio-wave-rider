Curator-driven backtest — 2021-03-31 to 2026-03-31

The watchlist-curator agent was called quarterly over a 5 year historical window. At each rebalance it read the news of the preceding quarter and proposed adds and removes against the active watchlist; the optimizer then ran mean-variance on the revised watchlist.
