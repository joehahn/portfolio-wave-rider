Curator-driven backtest

The watchlist-curator agent was called 28 times over a 1673-day window. At each rebalance it proposed adds and removes against the active watchlist; the optimizer then ran vanilla mean-variance on whatever set resulted. The two baselines below isolate the contribution of the curation (vs fixed watchlist same cadence) and of any rebalancing at all (vs buy-and-hold of the day-0 set).
