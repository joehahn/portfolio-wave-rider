Curator-driven backtest — 2021-03-31 to 2026-03-31

The watchlist-curator agent was called quarterly over a 5 year historical window. At each rebalance it read the news of the preceding quarter and proposed adds and removes against the active watchlist; the optimizer then ran mean-variance on the revised watchlist. The buy-and-hold curve below is the value of the initial portfolio (which never gets rebalanced or optimized) over time. The starter watchlist is [AAPL, MSFT, GOOGL, SPY, NVDA], a plausible portfolio for a tech-aware US investor in early 2021. The buy-and-hold baseline is the equal-weight allocation (20% in each of the five tickers) bought on day 0 and held without rebalancing through the end of the window.
