Curator-driven backtest — 2022-03-31 to 2025-10-31

The watchlist-curator agent was called quarterly over a 5 year historical window. At each rebalance it read the news of the preceding quarter and proposed adds and removes against the active watchlist; the optimizer then ran mean-variance on the revised watchlist. Each rebalance is marked by an orange square on the curator curve in chart 1 — hover over one to see that quarter's adds, removes, and the curator's rationale. The buy-and-hold curve below is the value of the initial portfolio (which never gets rebalanced or optimized) over time. The buy-and-hold portfolio has equal amounts of [AAPL, MSFT, GOOGL, NVDA, SPY] and is held without any rebalancing across the 5 year window. Throughout the charts below: general_markets = defensive equity ETFs (broad-market / dividend / utilities / staples); cashlike = bonds + cash-equivalents + precious metals (e.g., AGG, BIL, IAU).
