Shorter lookbacks (≤ 1 year) chase recent momentum: the μ estimate weighs the most recent surges heavily, so newly accelerating waves get more weight, but the resulting weights are noisier. Longer lookbacks smooth out short-term swings at the cost of diluting recent dynamics. SPY benchmark return over the same window: +27.54%.

Ticker-universe note: a ticker is included only if it has at least LB years of price history at the start of the backtest.
